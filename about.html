ABOUT EE GAMES

Who we are and how we keep games free.

OUR MISSION

Build awesome games.

We build games for the web and for mobile (iOS and Android). Whether you're on a phone or a desktop, our games should be accessible.

We thought it'd be fun to create this website to drive traffic to our site and maybe get some more app downloads.

We also don't collect data on this site.

Our browser games require nothing — no login, no download. Our mobile apps are free to download on the App Store and Google Play.

HOW WE STAY FREE

Optional ads on our mobile games is our current approach. Maybe someday we'll make a paid game.

  • Our mobile apps include optional ads served by Google AdMob.
  • Ads are OPTIONAL. Each game can be played without seeing a single ad, but offer help if you get stuck
  • Our browser games on this site are completely ad-free.

If you play our mobile apps, the ads you see are what make it possible for us to keep building and releasing new games at no cost to you (of course, your time is a cost...). We appreciate it.

For more details on what data the ad network may collect, see our Privacy Policy.

CONTACT

Questions, feedback, or just want to say hi? Reach us at support@ee-games.com or visit our Support page.
